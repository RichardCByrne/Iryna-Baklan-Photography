Iryna Baklan (b. 1995) is a Dublin based photographer from Switzerland.
With an interest in sociology, Iryna likes to explore her shared environment employing photography as a tool to document and reflect on her findings. Intrigued by the juncture of man and artifice, she looks at how space is formed and organised, gradually assembling shapes and patterns that form the patchwork of our cultural landscape.
